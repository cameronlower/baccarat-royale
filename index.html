How to Play

The Objective

Bet on whether the Player or Banker hand will total closest to 9, or predict a Tie. You are not the Player — you simply wager on an outcome.

Card Values

  • Ace = 1 point
  • 2 through 9 = face value
  • 10, J, Q, K = 0 points

Only the last digit of the total counts — a hand of 7 + 8 = 15 scores as 5.

Naturals (Instant Win)

If either hand totals 8 or 9 on the first two cards, it's a Natural. No further cards are drawn and the highest Natural wins.

Third Card Rules

  • Player draws a third card on totals 0–5; stands on 6 or 7.
  • Banker drawing follows standard Punto Banco rules based on the Banker's total and the Player's third card value.

Payouts

  • Player wins → 1 : 1
  • Banker wins → 0.95 : 1 (5% house commission)
  • Tie → 8 : 1 — Player & Banker bets push (stakes returned)
  • Player / Banker Pair → 11 : 1 (first two cards of that hand form a pair)
